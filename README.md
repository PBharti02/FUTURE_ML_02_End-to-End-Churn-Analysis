KPIs Walkthrough:
-
‚Ä¢	High Overall Churn: The company faces a significant churn problem, with a Total Churn Rate of 27.0% (1,732 customers out of 6,418).
‚Ä¢	Contract Risk is Critical: Customers on Month-to-month contracts are the most vulnerable segment, accounting for the highest historical churn rate (46.5%) and dominating the predicted churn pool (355 out of 378 predicted churners).
‚Ä¢	Geographical Hotspot: The state of Jammu exhibits an alarmingly high churn rate of 57.2%, making it the most critical geographic area for intervention.
‚Ä¢	Service Adoption and Retention: Customers who do not use a Device Protection Plan have the highest churn rate among service usage categories at 71.0%, suggesting this service may be key for retention.
‚Ä¢	Prediction Focus: The model predicts 378 customers are currently at risk of churning, who collectively generate 42,603.54 in total revenue, highlighting the financial value of the targeted retention efforts.




Business Impact & Insights:
-
Various parts of the telecom sector can utilize the dashboard to retain more customers and make the business more profitable:
Financial Impact: They see the Total Churn Rate (27.0%) and the Total Revenue of Predicted Churners ($\$42,603.54$). This guides resource allocation, showing the financial priority of retention efforts vs. acquisition.

This allows them to craft personalized, high-value offers (e.g., contract upgrades, loyalty discounts) instead of mass marketing.
